# 🌸 Bloom — Deployment Guide
## From Code to Google Play Store

---

## 📁 YOUR FILES CHECKLIST

Make sure you have all these files in one folder:
- ✅ index.html        ← your main app
- ✅ manifest.json     ← PWA settings
- ✅ sw.js             ← service worker (offline support)
- ✅ icon.svg          ← convert this to icon-192.png and icon-512.png
- 📷 icon-192.png      ← you need to create this (see Step 1)
- 📷 icon-512.png      ← you need to create this (see Step 1)

---

## STEP 1 — Create Your App Icons

You need two PNG versions of the icon:

Option A (easiest — free):
1. Go to https://cloudconvert.com/svg-to-png
2. Upload your icon.svg file
3. Set size to 192x192 → download → save as icon-192.png
4. Repeat with size 512x512 → save as icon-512.png

Option B (also free):
1. Go to https://realfavicongenerator.net
2. Upload icon.svg
3. Download the full icon package

---

## STEP 2 — Deploy to Netlify (free hosting)

1. Go to https://netlify.com and sign up free
2. On the dashboard click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
3. Drag and drop your ENTIRE FOLDER (with all files) onto the page
4. Netlify gives you a live link like: https://bloom-yourname.netlify.app
5. TEST IT on your phone browser — you should see the install banner! 🎉

---

## STEP 3 — Test Your PWA

On Android phone:
1. Open your Netlify link in Chrome
2. After a few seconds you'll see "Add Bloom to Home Screen" banner
3. Tap Install
4. Bloom appears on your home screen like a real app! 📱

On iPhone (Safari only):
1. Open your Netlify link in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (□↑)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap Add — done! 🌸

---

## STEP 4 — Wrap for Google Play Store

1. Go to https://www.pwabuilder.com (free tool)
2. Paste your Netlify URL into the search box
3. Click "Package for stores"
4. Select "Google Play Store"
5. Fill in your app details:
   - App name: Bloom
   - Package name: com.yourname.bloom
   - Version: 1.0.0
6. Click "Generate Package"
7. Download the APK/AAB file

---

## STEP 5 — Submit to Google Play Store

1. Go to https://play.google.com/console
2. Sign up for a Google Play Developer account
3. Pay the ONE-TIME $25 registration fee
4. Click "Create app"
5. Fill in app details:
   - App name: Bloom — Your Daily Companion
   - Category: Health & Fitness or Productivity
   - Content rating: Everyone
6. Upload your AAB file from Step 4
7. Add screenshots (take them from your phone)
8. Write your app description
9. Submit for review
10. Wait 1–3 days → YOUR APP IS ON THE PLAY STORE! 🎉
